# Legal Positioning Notes

## Product framing

Ross is a productivity tool for advocates. It is not positioned as:

- A substitute for advocate judgment
- Public-facing legal advice
- An `AI lawyer`
- A lead-generation or solicitation platform

## Output language

- `Draft for advocate review`
- `Source-backed output`
- `Not found in the case file`
- `Designed to keep case files on your device`

## Claims to avoid

- `100% private`
- `Fully DPDP compliant`
- `Guaranteed legal accuracy`
- `Legal advice`

## Source policy

- Use official, public, or licensed public-law sources
- Do not scrape paywalled sources without permission
- Do not represent public-law snippets as private case findings
