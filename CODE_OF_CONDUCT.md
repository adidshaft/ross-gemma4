# Code of Conduct

Ross Gemma4 contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, specific, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Assume privacy-sensitive data may exist around legal workflows and handle examples carefully.
- Welcome contributors with different experience levels, locations, languages, and legal-system backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, case data, secrets, or credentials.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Using the project to encourage unsupervised legal automation or harmful legal outcomes.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate these expectations.
